FAQ about Decor

Q: How can I request a specific theme for the decor?

A: You can specify your desired theme during the initial consultation. Our team will work with you to create a customized decor plan based on your preferences.

Q: Do you provide setup and takedown services for decor?

A: Yes, our decor services include both setup and takedown. Our team will ensure that the venue is beautifully decorated before the event and will handle the removal afterward.

Q: Can I add personalized elements to the decor?

A: Absolutely! We welcome personalization. You can add special touches like photos, banners, or specific colors to make the decor uniquely yours.

Q: What types of venues do you decorate?

A: We can decorate various venues, including indoor and outdoor spaces, banquet halls, and private residences. Our goal is to transform any space into a visually stunning environment.

FAQ about Catering

Q: What types of cuisine do you offer for catering?

A: We offer a diverse range of cuisines, including but not limited to, Italian, Mexican, Asian, and traditional Ethiopian dishes. Our menu can be customized to suit your preferences.

Q: Can you accommodate special dietary restrictions or allergies?

A: Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions and allergies. Please inform us of any specific requirements during the consultation, and our chef will work to create a menu that meets your needs.

FAQ about Cake

Q: Can I request a custom-designed cake based on a specific theme or character?

A: Yes, we offer custom-designed cakes that can be tailored to your preferred theme, whether it's based on a character, color scheme, or specific design. During the consultation, share your ideas, and our team will create a unique cake for your celebration.

Q: What types of snacks are included in your snack providers' offerings?

A: Our snack providers offer a diverse range of snacks, including savory and sweet options. You can expect a delightful assortment of finger foods, candies, and treats to suit different tastes and preferences.

Q: How far in advance should I place an order for a custom cake or snacks?

A: We recommend placing custom cake orders at least two weeks in advance to ensure ample time for design and preparation. For snack providers, it's advisable to confirm your order a week before the event to secure the availability of your chosen snacks.

Q: Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions for cakes and snacks?

A: Absolutely! We understand the importance of catering to various dietary needs. Whether you have gluten-free, vegan, or other specific requirements, let us know during the ordering process, and we'll work to provide suitable options for your celebration.
